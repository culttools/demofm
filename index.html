ABOUT THE SIGNAL

DEMO FM is community radio infrastructure. We broadcast music, politics, and joy -- not because they're separate things, but because they never were.

Inspired by low-power FM activism and sound system culture, we build local signal as political practice. Every broadcast is an act of collective imagination. Every listener is a participant, not a consumer.
